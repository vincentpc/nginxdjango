About poll app

Online poll app is a django powered polling application influenced by Abu Ashraf Masnun's recent tutorial and official tutorial from Django's site. The app does a fairly simple job :). User will select a poll , choose an answer and vote it. An immediate result will be displayed just after the vote. The app uses django's builtin admin app and hook the poll app in it to provide an interface to perform CRUID operation on the app.
